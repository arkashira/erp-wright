# Business Model Canvas for erp-wright
## Value Proposition
* **AI-driven automation** for legacy ERP systems, reducing manual data entry and order processing time by up to 70%
* **Unified backend systems**, connecting to 20+ legacy ERPs via secure, no-downtime adapters
* **Low-code workflow customization**, enabling factory floor managers to build and deploy process automations without writing code
* **Built for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs)**, with a focus on resource-constrained production environments

## Customer Segments
* **Small and medium-sized manufacturers** with <500 employees and limited IT staff
* **Emerging-market manufacturing** companies, particularly in Thai and Vietnamese industrial zones
* **Factory floor managers** and operational teams seeking to automate repetitive tasks and improve efficiency

## Channels
* **Direct sales** and account management for large and strategic customers
* **Digital marketing** and online advertising to reach a wider audience of SMEs
* **Partnerships** with industry associations, consulting firms, and system integrators to expand reach and credibility
* **Word-of-mouth referrals** and case studies to build trust and credibility among potential customers

## Revenue Streams
* **Subscription-based licensing** for the erp-wright platform, with tiered pricing based on the number of users, features, and support levels
* **Professional services** for implementation, customization, and training, offered on a project basis or as part of a premium support package
* **Maintenance and support** fees for ongoing software updates, security patches, and technical assistance

## Cost Structure
* **Development and engineering** costs for building and maintaining the erp-wright platform
* **Sales and marketing** expenses for acquiring new customers and promoting the platform
* **Infrastructure and hosting** costs for supporting the platform and its users
* **Customer support and success** costs for ensuring customer satisfaction and retention

## Key Resources
* **Development team** with expertise in AI, automation, and ERP systems
* **Sales and marketing team** with experience in targeting SMEs and emerging-market manufacturers
* **Infrastructure and hosting** capabilities to support the platform and its users
* **Partnerships and alliances** with industry associations, consulting firms, and system integrators

## Key Activities
* **Platform development and maintenance** to ensure the erp-wright platform remains competitive and meets customer needs
* **Sales and marketing efforts** to acquire new customers and promote the platform
* **Customer support and success** activities to ensure customer satisfaction and retention
* **Partnership development and management** to expand reach and credibility

## Key Partners
* **Industry associations** and trade groups to promote the platform and build credibility
* **Consulting firms** and system integrators to expand reach and offer complementary services
* **Technology partners** to leverage their expertise and capabilities in AI, automation, and ERP systems
* **Strategic investors** to provide funding and guidance for growth and expansion
